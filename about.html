About Us

Less Carbon Footprint(zacook), More Impact

Our mission is to empower organizations to lead the global transition to a low-carbon economy. We deliver strategic consulting, carbon market expertise, and scalable solutions that turn climate ambition into actionable results.

Our Values

Integrity

We act transparently and uphold ethical standards in every engagement.

Impact

Our work is driven by real-world outcomes and measurable climate results.

Partnership

We build trust through collaboration and shared vision with every client and stakeholder.

Innovation

We adapt, iterate, and explore emerging tools to stay ahead in a fast-evolving climate landscape.

Equity

We support climate solutions that are inclusive and just across all regions we serve.

ZACOOK is a key player in the Korean carbon market, contributing to energy solutions and carbon reduction through carbon credit trading, project consulting, and project financing.

We specialize in project development, carbon trading, and capacity building. We support organizations in designing and implementing high-impact climate projects, navigating carbon markets, and strengthening institutional expertise. Our tailored services empower clients to drive sustainable change and meet global environmental goals.

Advisory Board

Young June Park

Senior Advisor

    Mr.Park is the current Chairman of the Ansei Foundation and brings extensive experience in public administration and economic policy. He has held key leadership roles in the Korean government, including 2nd Vice Minister of Trade, Industry and Energy, Deputy Minister for Government Policy Coordination at the Prime Minister’s Office, and Secretary for Policy Coordination in the Presidential Office.

Tae Yong Jung

Senior Advisor

    Professor Jung brings deep expertise in energy and climate policy, currently serving as Professor at Yonsei University's Graduate School of International Studies and Co-Chair of the K-Policy Platform. He has held influential roles in global development institutions, including as a Senior Energy Economist at the World Bank and formerly as the Lead Climate Change Specialist at the Asian Development Bank.

Sung Jin Kang

Senior Advisor

    Dr.Kang is a Professor in the Department of Economics at Korea University and a respected leader in the field of international and Korean economic research. He has served as President of both the Korea International Economic Association and the Korean Economic Association, and was previously an Associate Professor at the University of Tsukuba in Japan.

Namjoon Cho

Senior Advisor

    Dr. Nam-Joon Cho is the MRS-Singapore Chair Professor at Nanyang Technological University and Director of the Center for Cross Economy. A graduate of Stanford University, he leads antiviral drug discovery efforts at Stanford’s SyneRx AViDD Center and is internationally recognized for his research in biomaterials and biomedical engineering. His work has led to over 280 publications and multiple biotech innovations.

DH Kim

Senior Advisor

    Mr.Dohyung (DH) Kim is the Founder and CEO of Finhaven Group, a fintech company focused on innovating global capital markets through blockchain-based platforms. He is a member of the British Columbia Securities Commission’s Fintech Advisory Forum and recipient of CEO Monthly’s 2020 Global Capital Markets CEO of the Year award. Previously, he founded NMC Resource Corporation and began his career in investment banking at Merrill Lynch in New York.
